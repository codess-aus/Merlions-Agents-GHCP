## 🎬 Demo discipline
- **Mute clip audio.** Narrate live.
- **First sentence after each clip is rehearsed.** Don't ad-lib the handoff.
- **Fallback screenshots ready** for every clip — point and narrate the same beats.

## 🪜 Three takeaway anchors (Slide 12)
1. **Specialise** your agents
2. **Build trust in**, not on
3. **Ship to Azure** with observability **from day one**

## 🦁 Power lines (use sparingly, land each one)
- "Smaller agent, smaller blast radius."
- "Personality is a feature. Trust is what lets you ship it."
- "Refusal is a feature."
- "We log decisions, not content."
- "Observable systems are trustworthy systems."
- "Lah, let's build."

## 🆘 If something breaks

| Problem | Do this |
|---|---|
| Demo clip won't play | Switch to fallback screenshots, narrate same beats |
| Slides freeze | Walk to front of stage, tell the next story from memory, signal AV |
| Mic dies | Project from the diaphragm, move closer to front row |
| Audience cold | Skip to the pun on Slide 9 early, recover energy |
| You're 3+ min over at S10 | Cut the practical tips list on S10, go straight to demo 3 |
